Tapenade (olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and its always a big hit at parties. My father reommends

"Make this the night before so that the flavors time to blend. Just bring it up to room temputure before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm"

Ingrediants

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2/3 large garlic cloves
  • 1 t. capers

instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend unil smooth as possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. pulse themotor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture
Serve on thin rounds with goat cheese and fresg basil garnish (optional).
